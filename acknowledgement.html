Difficult Technique Support

During the project, we had a lot of problems, and thanks to the help of these teachers, so that we can successfully complete our project.

Prof. Xinkui Wang
We decided to use AuNPs to verify whether the aptamer binds to kanamycin, so we asked Prof. Wang for help on how to prepare AuNPs and improving our experimental protocol.

Prof. Mahinur Akkaya
The preparation of the circular template is the most important part of our experiment, but we don't know exactly how to separate the prepared circular template. Thanks to Prof. Akkaya for helping us solve this problem.

Prof. Dongsheng Liu
For DNA hydrogels, we know very little about it, so we asked experts in this field for help. Prof. Liu provided us with a variety of methods to detect the degree of hydrogel formation.
