1.Prepare hydrogels through RCA Add 20μl of annular template and 20μl of Aptamer-Primer 1 to a centrifuge tube with 160μL TE solution, mix and incubate them at room temperature for 2 hours.
2. The R-process Divide 100 ul solution evenly into 2 centrifuge tube, 100ul of reaction solution and PBS solution were added respectively to two tubes. Then, add 20ul of dNTP, 10ul pf φ29 buffer and 1ul of φ29. After the mixing, add 115ul of the mixture into 96-well plate and let it incubate at 30 °C for 40min.
3. The M-Process: 1 ul of primer 2 and primer 3 were added to each well and then incubate at 30 ° C for 16 h.
